UX/UI Proyects During 8 weeks I developed 6 proyects that I'm going to show you all.

CreArte

Is a web platform that helps artist to monetize their content. Giving the user the option to organize their own contest and / or exhibition gives many beginner artists the opportunity to choose this route. The artists will make themselves known and try to win those monetary prizes. There will also be other types of contact on the web, if someone is interested in the content you upload, they can contact you to collaborate or to buy your production. In addition the website will also serve as your portfolio and you will find a section of recommendations according to a test of interests that we do when you register for the first time. There are no more excuses, create art, create yourself.

Medium: CreArte - Week 7 and 8th

Chill

Is a tool for people who suffer social anxiety to work on their condition and accept it. That way they can deal with those situations in a more regular way. It has 3 main sections; the first one consist on doing mini challenges that the user will decide itself, then you have a relaxation mode reachable when the user needs to keep their calm or get it, and the last one is meant to collect all the information and progress you are making with Chill.
